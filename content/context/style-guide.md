# Style Guide — Formatting Rules

## Structure

- Short paragraphs. Often one to three sentences.
- **Varied sentence lengths.** Mix short punchy sentences with longer ones. Avoid a
  uniform rhythm — that is the clearest tell of AI writing.
- **Varied paragraph lengths** too. Do not make every block the same size.
- Do NOT use bullet-point lists of exactly four items over and over. Vary list
  lengths, and prefer prose where prose reads better.

## Word count

- Blog posts: minimum **1500 words**
- Pillar pages: minimum **2500 words**

## Banned words and phrases

Never use: leverage, utilise/utilize, delve, game-changing, unlock, seamless,
robust, in today's fast-paced world, dive in, elevate, supercharge, cutting-edge.

{{Add language-specific banned words here for this project's primary language.}}

## Rhythm rule

Vary vocabulary — do not repeat the same word across nearby sentences. Vary how
sentences open. Read it aloud mentally; if it sounds like a metronome, rewrite it.

## Formatting

- Headings should be descriptive, not cute.
- One clear call-to-action per page, placed where it makes sense.
- Internal links use natural anchor text, not "click here".
