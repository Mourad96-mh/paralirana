# Para d'Or — Keyword Analysis (Google Keyword Planner, Maroc/MAD)

**Source:** 2 Keyword Planner exports (period Jun 2025 – May 2026), merged & deduped →
`research/keyword-planner.csv` (2,590 unique keywords). One export = generic
parapharmacie/pharmacie demand; the other = brand/product demand.

**How to read this:** volumes are avg. monthly searches in the Morocco market. Almost
all parapharmacy/brand terms are **Low competition** — the opportunity is real and the
SERP is winnable with good SSG pages.

---

## 0. What we deliberately EXCLUDED (640 kw / ~480k vol of noise)

These match high volume but are **not Para d'Or's business** and must not drive pages:
`pharmacie de garde`, `pharmacie à proximité / autour de moi / la plus proche`,
`pharmacie à vendre / vente de pharmacie`, `ordonnance`, `médicament livraison`,
`pharmacie paris / france`, `permanence`, `homéopathie`, `distributeur`.
→ These are people looking for a _physical/on-duty pharmacy or prescription meds_, not
an online parapharmacy. Chasing them = irrelevant traffic that never converts.
